How to Use Sparx Tutor

  • Click on "Extract Text" to capture the story text from the webpage
  • Click on "Answer" to paste the story and question to the AI chat.
  • Use the AI to get detailed explanations of the content
  • If you do not have an OpenAI API key, you will need to extract the text and questions manually into another AI.
    Ex: ChatGPT, MetaAI, etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the text being extracted?
Ensure that the webpage has fully loaded. If content loads dynamically, try refreshing the page and running the tool again.
How do I use the AI Chat?
Simply click "Extract Text" to capture the content then click "Answer" on the question page. The AI will provide detailed explanations based on the extracted content. (Repeat For Each Question)
How can I use the AI?
Head to the GPT4.1.py File and insert your OpenAI API key. Afterward, you can interact with the AI Tutor by asking questions about the extracted content.
How Accurate is GPT-4.1?
GPT-4.1 was tested with a 98% accuracy rate on Sparx Reader questions. Although keep in mind that it may not always be perfect.
